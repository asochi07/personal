Ekenechukwu Ofuokwu
Head Boy

Head Boy


I have come to realize that getting the post is the easiest part of it all, the main task lies in how well I discharge my duties. This leadership position has given me the ability to make massive decisions that would affect the students. Acting in this capacity, you cannot allow your emotions to compromise anything you do and that means you need to be fair in all your actions. I never forget to put God first in everything I do because without Him I do not think I would be where I am today. I will always remember that Atlantic Hall trusted me with this position because they have faith in my abilities and I will not let them down.
